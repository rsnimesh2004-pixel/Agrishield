    Prevention

    Stay Ahead of Disease

    Proactive farming practices that reduce disease risk and protect your yield.

    💧

    Water at Ground Level

    Avoid overhead watering which wets leaves and creates humid conditions ideal for fungal growth. Use drip irrigation when possible.

    🔄

    Rotate Your Crops

    Alternate crop families each season to break disease and pest cycles. A 3-year rotation plan dramatically reduces soil-borne pathogens.

    ✂️

    Remove Infected Plants

    Promptly remove and destroy (don't compost) infected plant material. This prevents spores from spreading to healthy neighbors.

    📅

    Scout Weekly

    Walk your fields weekly and inspect leaves top and bottom. Early detection is the single most powerful disease management tool.

    🌬️

    Improve Air Circulation

    Space plants properly and prune dense growth. Good airflow reduces humidity around leaves, a key driver of fungal diseases.

    🌱

    Use Resistant Varieties

    Choose disease-resistant seed varieties certified for your region. Check local agricultural extension services for recommendations.
